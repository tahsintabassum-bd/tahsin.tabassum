Hi! I am Tahsin!

I am a graduate student at The Oklahoma University (OU), currently working as a graduate assistant in the Department of Regional and City Planning, OU.

My research focus lies in exploring transportation equity, public transit, and vulnerable road user safety. Through data-driven methodologies integrating spatial data analysis, statistical techniques, and machine learning modeling, I aim to address critical urban mobility challenges and promote equitable and safe transportation systems.
